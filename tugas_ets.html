About FC Barcelona

FC Barcelona is one of the most iconic football clubs in the world, based in Barcelona, Spain. Known by its famous motto “Més que un club” (More than a club), Barça represents more than football — it stands for identity, culture, and passion.

  • Founded in 1899.
  • Home stadium: Camp Nou.
  • Famous for its Blaugrana colors and attacking style.

Barcelona is more than just a team. It is a global symbol of excellence in football, producing legendary players and embracing a unique philosophy through La Masia academy. With millions of fans worldwide, Barça continues to inspire generations.

Top Management

Our Top Management

Joan Laporta

President

Joan Laporta is the current president of FC Barcelona, having been elected in March 2021. He previously served as president from 2003 to 2010, overseeing one of the most successful periods in the club's history, including multiple La Liga and UEFA Champions League titles.

Rafa Yuste

Vice President

Rafa Yuste is the current vice president of FC Barcelona, having been elected alongside Joan Laporta in March 2021. He is a former footballer and sports executive, known for his work in player recruitment and development. As vice president, Yuste plays a key role in shaping the club's sporting strategy and overseeing the football operations.

Hansi Flick

Head Coach

Hansi Flick is the current head coach of FC Barcelona, having taken over the position in 2021. He is a former footballer and coach, known for his tactical approach and ability to develop young talent. As head coach, Flick is responsible for the team's performance on the pitch and plays a crucial role in the club's overall strategy.

Deco

Sporting Director

Deco is the current sporting director of FC Barcelona, having been appointed in 2021. He is a former professional footballer who played as an attacking midfielder, known for his creativity and vision on the field. As sporting director, Deco is responsible for overseeing the club's player recruitment, development, and overall sporting strategy.
